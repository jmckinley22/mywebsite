About me

Hi! My name is Jackson McKinley and this is my personal website to share who I am. I am originally from Southeast Connecticut, and grew up sailing on Long Island Sound with an affinity for the ocean. My love for the water has endured throughout my life, taking me all the way to Los Angelesv where I study Applied Mathematics at the University of Southern California and compete on the sailing team. I am an avid learner and creator, always looking for new ways to expand my knowledge and skillset through projects, both physical and digital. In the past I have elevated to various leadership roles, both in my extracurriculars and my professional work, and look forward to more opportuntities to learn and gain valuable life experience.
